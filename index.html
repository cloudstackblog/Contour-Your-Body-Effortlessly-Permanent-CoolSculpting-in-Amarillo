What is the procedure like in American Laser Med Spa?

At American Laser Med Spa, we take the time to discuss a holistic approach to your goals for reshaping your body. Together, we’ll look at your whole body from different angles – and then focus on specific areas where you want to eliminate fat.

During the procedure, an applicator will first be administered to the targeted areas, allowing for the tissue to be drawn into the vacuum cup. The cooling process will then take place, causing a decrease in the temperature of the fat cells, leading to their permanent damage.

Over the course of the next few weeks, your body’s natural immune system will work towards expelling the damaged fat cells. The procedure generally takes about an hour and causes minimal discomfort, with most patients enjoying a good book or a nap to pass the time.

How CoolSculpting Works: The Science Behind the Freeze

CoolSculpting uses a technology known as Cryolipolysis, which selectively targets and freezes fat cells in specific areas of the body. The process is both safe and effective, allowing you to achieve noticeable fat reduction without damaging surrounding tissue. Here’s how the treatment works:

Step-by-Step Process of CoolSculpting

  1. Consultation: Your CoolSculpting journey begins with a consultation at American Laser Med Spa. During this meeting, the CoolSculpting specialist will assess your areas of concern and discuss your goals for body contouring. Together, you’ll determine if CoolSculpting is the right treatment for you and create a customized treatment plan.
  2. Application: During the procedure, a gel pad is applied to the treatment area, followed by the CoolSculpting applicator. The applicator uses controlled cooling to freeze and target the fat cells in the selected area. You’ll feel a cold sensation at first, which gradually numbs the area. Most patients find the procedure comfortable and relaxing.
  3. Fat cell elimination: After the treatment, the fat cells are gradually broken down and naturally eliminated by your body over the following weeks and months. As the fat cells are eliminated, your body begins to look more toned and sculpted.
